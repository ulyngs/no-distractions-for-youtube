Privacy Policy

I (Ulrik Lyngs) built `Hide distractions on YouTube` as a Safari Web Extension which does only this: hides recommended videos on the front page of YouTube, 'related videos' appearing when watching a video, and (on Mac and iPad) hides comments and adds a button that loads them when clicked/tapped.

The extension collects zero data.

If you have any questions or suggestions, contact me at ulrik.lyngs@gmail.com.
